Mission
Why EcoSort exists

Most waste in Central Asian cities ends up in the wrong bin — not from laziness, but from lack of clear information. We fix that.

🏙️

Built for Almaty

Designed with local recycling infrastructure in mind. We map real collection points and partner with city waste services.

🔬

Transparent AI

No black boxes. The classifier shows confidence scores and explains its reasoning. You always know why a decision was made.

🌱

Real impact

Each correctly sorted item reduces landfill load and CO₂ emissions. We report community impact monthly with verifiable data.
